PvP Basics

PvP stands for player-vs-player, which means you get to battle another Pokémon Go player. You bring a team of 3 pokémon to fight against 3 of their pokémon. You can do the following moves during the battle:

  1. Fast Attacks: Tap anywhere on the screen to use your fast attack. The energy generated by each hit of your fast attack will fill your charge move button.
  2. Charged Attacks: Once you have gained enough energy, the icon will light up, and you can unleash a powerful attack on your opponent.
  3. Shields: Each player gets 2 at the beginning of every game, and they can be used to block an incoming charge move. Deciding when and when not to use these shields will be very important on your road to victory.
  4. Swapping: This will bring up a menu to show your remaining Pokémon. You can use this to change out your current Pokémon for one that might do better. Beware: you can only swap once every 60 seconds.

The Silph League Arena

The Silph League Arena creates monthly themed tournaments, which restricts the pokémon you can use in battles. Pokémon Go communities across the globe organize tournaments using the rules imposed by the Silph Arena. All the information about these tournaments are recorded in the site so that players can see how they rank against other players all over the world.
